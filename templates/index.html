AI Readiness Checklist

Welcome to the AI Readiness Checklist for Organizations

Your comprehensive guide to evaluating and preparing for successful AI adoption. This checklist is designed to help organizations assess their capabilities, identify gaps, and take actionable steps toward integrating AI responsibly and effectively.

How to Use This Checklist

  • Many activities may occur in parallel (e.g., governance frameworks + data infrastructure).
  • Sections follow best practices, from foundational elements to execution and improvement.
  • Tailor the checklist to your organization’s needs and goals.

Think of this checklist as a roadmap rather than a strict timeline. It’s designed to guide you through the complex journey of AI implementation while allowing flexibility.

A Logical Flow with Flexibility

The checklist is divided into 10 major sections, each addressing a critical area of AI readiness:

  1. Strategy and Vision – Establish your purpose and goals for AI.
  2. Data Preparedness – Ensure your data is ready for AI initiatives.
  3. Technology and Infrastructure – Equip your organization with the necessary tools.
  4. Governance, Compliance, and Ethics – Build the foundation for responsible AI use.
  5. Workforce and Culture – Prepare your people for AI adoption.
  6. Implementation and Operations – Transition from planning to action.
  7. Risk and Security – Safeguard your systems and address vulnerabilities.
  8. Financial and ROI Preparedness – Evaluate costs and measure success.
  9. Continuous Learning and Innovation – Stay adaptable and forward-thinking.

This structure allows you to tackle AI readiness systematically while understanding that some areas will naturally overlap.
